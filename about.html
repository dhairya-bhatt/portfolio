Who I am?


From a young age, Ive been fascinated by how technology and math intersect to solve real-world problems. This curiosity drove me to author a general interest math book during the COVID-19 lockdown, exploring topics from cryptocurrencies to the four color theorem. I have also been actively involved in creating and leading organizations that aim to make a difference, such as the Bhavnagar Maths Championships and Bhavnagar.art.

When Im not immersed in academics or my projects, I enjoy staying active and engaged through sports. Football is my primary sport, but I also appreciate playing golf, badminton, and squash. Each sport offers its unique challenges and joys, contributing to my overall well-being and balance.

Music is another significant aspect of my life. Whether its through listening to my favorite tracks of Radiohead and Lana Del Rey or discovering new genres, music provides a much-needed respite from my busy schedule and enhances my creativity.

In addition to my interests in technology, mathematics, and sports, I have a strong inclination towards studying geography. Understanding the dynamics of our planet and its diverse cultures adds another layer of depth to my educational journey.

I invite you to explore my projects and achievements to gain insight into my dedication and the impact I aim to make. My journey is a blend of passion, innovation, and a continuous quest for knowledge, and Im excited to share it with you.
